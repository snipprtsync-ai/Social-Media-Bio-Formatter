How the Bio Architect works

        
Bio Architect is a safe sandbox for creators, influencers, and small business owners who want to perfect their social media bios before publishing them.

        
    
          
  • Draft once, paste anywhere. The editor mirrors your text into Instagram-style and TikTok-style mobile previews in real time.
    • 
          
  • Respect your line breaks. Use invisible characters so Instagram and TikTok stop collapsing your spacing.
    • 
          
  • Stay on the safe side of limits. A character counter and progress bar help you stay within Instagram's 150-character guideline.

  • Autosave built-in. Your draft is stored in your browser with localStorage, so refreshes and crashes do not wipe your work.
